LUVOIR: Coronagraphic Spectra of Earth-like Planets

This is a basic presentation of simulated spectra for Earth-like planets.
Choose your exposure time, planet distance, and radius. Remember, this is a *prototype*.

The underlying model is derived from the python-based version of Tyler Robinson's coronagraphic spectrum and noise model. Python by Jacob Lustig-Yaeger, Bokeh rendering by Jason Tumlinson.
